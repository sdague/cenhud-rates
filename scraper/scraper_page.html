Gas & Electric Supply Prices

When comparing offers from Energy Service companies (ESCOs), it may be helpful to use the Public Service Commission’s Power to Choose website which includes the Merchant Function Charges which are not listed below. The price below reflects Central Hudson's supply charges for electricity which consist of the Market Price Charge and Market Price Adjustment and natural gas which consists of the Gas Supply Charge. Bear in mind, in addition to Merchant Function Charges the prices given here also do not include energy delivery charges, which are regulated, stable, and apply whether you receive your energy supply from Central Hudson or an ESCO. Also, Central Hudson does not profit from energy supply costs or our energy purchasing services. Payments toward the supply portion of your energy bill are passed along to the companies that provide the supply of gas and electricity that Central Hudson delivers.
